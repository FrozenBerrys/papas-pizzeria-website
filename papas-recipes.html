Old Soul

$15.99

If you've worked at the Pizzeria for a summer job, you probably remember a funny fella named Wally... All part-timers deal with the existential dread of knowing there's a man out there who orders just 8 anchovies... those 4 cuts feel like an eternity. We started scheming, every Monday at 8:45, his pizza would be ready in the oven with olive juice and bits of sausage plants on top. We did this for 3 months. The man was basically eating an alpha version of the Old Soul at this point. He was the first to try it, and immediately died from a heart attack. Which is where the name Old Soul comes from anyways. It's a great combination for the savory-inclined, we recommend our customers to try it once to honor the old soul Wally.
